### 1. Breast Cancer Diagnosis Data
This dataset represents the diagnosis and the classifications of the tumors based on different metrics. 

- Malignant tumors: 
    - Cancerous growths
    - Can spread to other parts of the body 
    - More aggressive and dangerous
    - Represented by "M" in the dataset

- Benign tumors:
    - Non-cancerous growths
    - Do not spread to other body parts
    - Generally less dangerous
    - Represented by "B" in the dataset 

- **id**: Unique identifier for the case  
- **M/B**: Classification of the tumor as Malignant (M) or Benign (B)  
- **radius**: Mean distance from the center to points on the perimeter  
- **texture**: Standard deviation of gray-scale values  
- **perimeter**: Tumor perimeter length  
- **area**: Tumor area  
- **smoothness**: Local variation in radius lengths  
- **compactness**: Perimeter² / area - 1.0  
- **concavity**: Severity of concave portions of the contour  
- **concave points**: Number of concave portions of the contour  
- **symmetry**: Symmetry of the tumor  
- **fractal dimension**: "Coastline approximation" - 1
